4 Ordering Process

Our manufacturing process follows a structured workflow to ensure quality and transparency:

1

Enquiry & Quotation

Submit your requirements (product type, quantity, design, fabric preferences) via our website form, email, or WhatsApp. We will provide a detailed quotation within 24–48 hours.

2

Design Approval

Share your artwork/design files in print-ready format (AI, PSD, PDF, PNG at 300 DPI minimum). Our team will create a mock-up for your approval. Production does not begin until design is approved in writing.

3

Sampling (Optional but Recommended)

We produce a physical sample for your review. Sample cost is adjusted against the bulk order. Sampling typically takes 7–15 business days.

4

Advance Payment

A 50% advance payment is required to initiate bulk production. Production begins only after advance payment is confirmed.

5

Production & Quality Check

Bulk manufacturing with multi-stage quality checks. We share production updates and photos during the process.

6

Balance Payment & Dispatch

The remaining 50% balance is due before dispatch. Once confirmed, orders are dispatched with tracking details shared via email/WhatsApp.

Important: An order is considered confirmed only after (a) design approval is received in writing, and (b) the 50% advance payment is received. Verbal agreements or WhatsApp confirmations without payment do not constitute a binding order.

8 Quality Assurance

Quality is the cornerstone of our manufacturing process. Every order undergoes:

  • Fabric Inspection: Incoming fabric is checked for GSM, color consistency, and defects before cutting
  • Cutting Accuracy: Patterns are verified against the approved size chart with ±0.5 inch tolerance
  • Stitching Quality: Each garment is inspected for seam strength, alignment, and finish
  • Print/Embroidery Check: Artwork placement, color accuracy, and finish are verified against the approved sample
  • Final Inspection: 100% inspection of finished garments before packaging, checking for loose threads, stains, sizing, and overall appearance
  • Packaging: Individual poly-packing with size labels, sorted by size and bundled for easy distribution

Despite rigorous checks, industry-standard tolerance of up to 2% defective pieces is considered acceptable in bulk garment manufacturing. Any defects beyond this threshold are addressed through our Refund & Return Policy.
