2 Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you submit a quote request, enquiry form, or communicate with us, we may collect:

  • Identity Information: Full name, designation, company/organization name
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone number, WhatsApp number, mailing/shipping address
  • Business Information: Company name, GST number, nature of business, order history
  • Order Information: Product type, quantity, design files, fabric preferences, size breakdowns, customization details
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, payment transaction IDs (collected for processing payments only; we do not store full credit/debit card numbers)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Website, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, referring URLs, click patterns
  • Location Data: Approximate geographic location derived from your IP address
  • Log Data: Access times, server logs, error reports

2.3 Information from Third-Party Sources

We may receive information about you from:

  • Social media platforms (Instagram, LinkedIn) when you interact with our profiles
  • Business referrals from existing clients or partners
  • Logistics and courier partners regarding delivery status

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

Processing and fulfilling manufacturing orders, providing quotes, managing production schedules, and coordinating sample deliveries.

Communication

Responding to enquiries, sending order updates, delivery notifications, and providing customer support via email, phone, or WhatsApp.

Payment Processing

Processing advance payments (50% upfront), balance payments, generating invoices, and maintaining financial records as required by Indian tax law.

Analytics & Improvement

Understanding Website usage patterns, improving user experience, optimizing our manufacturing processes, and enhancing our service offerings.

Marketing

Sending newsletters, promotional offers, new product announcements, and industry updates (with your consent; you may opt out at any time).

Legal Compliance

Complying with applicable Indian laws, regulations, legal processes, and government requests, including GST filing and financial audit requirements.

4 Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share necessary information with logistics partners (e.g., courier companies for delivery), payment processors, and email service providers (such as EmailJS) who assist in our operations. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • Manufacturing Partners: In cases where production is sub-contracted to our partner facilities in Kolkata or other locations, relevant order details may be shared to facilitate manufacturing.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, regulation, court order, or governmental authority, including compliance with the IT Act, 2000 and GST regulations.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the succeeding entity.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information for any other purpose with your explicit consent.

6 Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted through our Website is protected using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Access Controls: Personal data is accessible only to authorized personnel who require it for legitimate business purposes
  • Secure Storage: Client design files, order details, and financial records are stored in password-protected systems with regular backups
  • Staff Training: Our team is trained on data privacy best practices and confidentiality requirements
  • Regular Audits: We conduct periodic reviews of our data handling practices and security measures

Important: While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to maintaining the highest standards of protection achievable.

7 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including:

  • Order Records: Retained for a minimum of 8 years in compliance with Indian GST and Income Tax Act record-keeping requirements
  • Client Communication: Retained for the duration of the business relationship plus 3 years for follow-up and quality assurance purposes
  • Design Files: Client-submitted designs are retained for the duration of the order plus 6 months (unless the client requests earlier deletion)
  • Website Analytics: Aggregated analytics data is retained for up to 26 months
  • Newsletter Subscriptions: Until you unsubscribe or request removal
